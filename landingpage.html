Your Questions, Answered.

How is my car's value calculated?

We analyze your vehicle's make, model, year, mileage, condition, and current market trends to estimate its value.

Can I use this value when selling my car?

Yes. The estimate gives you a realistic market value to help you negotiate with buyers or dealerships.

How often do vehicle values change?

Market prices can change frequently due to supply, demand, seasonal trends, and economic conditions.

What information do I need?

Typically you'll need your vehicle's make, model, year, mileage, and condition. Additional details help improve accuracy.

Does accident history affect my car's value?

Yes. Vehicles with accident history generally have lower market values than similar vehicles with clean histories.

Can I compare multiple vehicles?

Yes. You can calculate values for different vehicles to compare ownership costs and market prices.
