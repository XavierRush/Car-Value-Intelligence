Frequently Asked

Your questions, answered.

How is my car's value calculated?

We combine your vehicle's make, model, age, mileage, and condition with several independent pricing sources and current market trends, then reconcile them into one estimate.

Can I use this value when selling my car?

Yes. You'll get trade-in, private-party, and dealer retail figures, so you have the right number no matter how you sell.

How often do vehicle values change?

Market prices shift with supply, demand, season, and the economy — we recommend re-checking your value every few months.

What information do I need?

Just your car's make, model, original price, age, mileage, and vehicle type — pick most of it from dropdowns to avoid typos.

Does accident history affect my car's value?

Yes — vehicles with accident history typically carry a lower value than comparable clean-history vehicles.

Can I compare multiple vehicles?

Yes — run as many estimates as you like and check your past queries anytime from the Past Valuations page.
